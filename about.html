My Story

Photography has always been my way of honoring connection. As a mom and storyteller at heart, I understand how quickly seasons change—and how important it is to hold onto them.

I started Taozijia Photography to create a calm, joy-filled experience for families. From welcoming a new baby to celebrating little milestones, I'm here to document the love, laughter, and in-between moments you'll never want to forget.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me spending time with my family, exploring the beautiful PNW, and sipping PNW coffee while planning my next adventure.
