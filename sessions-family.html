Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about family photography. I want this experience to be joyful and easy for your family.

Ask a Question →

What should we wear for our family session?

+
Dress comfortably in outfits that coordinate but don't match exactly. Soft, neutral tones and simple textures look best. Once booked, I will send you a wardrobe guide to help.

Where do sessions take place?

+
We can shoot at a scenic park, beach, or forest in the Seattle area, in your home, or in our natural light studio. We will discuss and choose the perfect spot together.

What if my kids don't cooperate?

+
Don't worry! My sessions are relaxed and play-based. We'll run, laugh, and play games to capture their true personalities without forcing them to look or smile.
