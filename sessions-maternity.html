Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about maternity sessions.

View All FAQs →

When is the best time to schedule my maternity session?

+
We recommend scheduling your maternity session between 28 and 32 weeks of pregnancy. At this stage, your belly is beautifully round, and you'll still feel comfortable enough to move around and enjoy the session.

What should I wear for my session?

+
Choose clothing that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Soft, neutral tones, flowing dresses, and textured fabrics photograph beautifully. Once booked, I will send you a styling guide to help you select the perfect wardrobe.

Can my partner and children be included?

+
Absolutely! I highly encourage including your partner and children. Maternity sessions are a celebration of your family's growing love, and capturing these relationships is a key part of the session.

Where do sessions take place?

+
We can shoot at a variety of locations, from scenic parks and beaches in the Seattle area to a cozy in-home session or our natural light studio. We will discuss and choose the perfect location together.

How do we book a session?

+
To secure your date, simply fill out the contact form. We will select a date, and once the session fee is paid and contract is signed, your session will be officially booked!
