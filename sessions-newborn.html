PREPARING FOR YOUR SESSION

Preparing for Your Session

A little preparation goes a long way. I'll guide you every step of the way so you can relax and enjoy this special time.

  • 🍼 Keep baby comfortable, fed, and cozy

    Feed baby 30 minutes before I arrive to ensure a deep, milk-drunk sleep.

  • 👕 Neutral outfits work beautifully

    Soft textures, neutral tones, and simple wraps photograph best.

  • 🌡️ Heat the space

    Keep the room warm (around 78°F) so baby stays warm when undressed.

  • ⏱️ Plan for extra time—babies lead the way

    We build in extra time for diaper changes, feedings, and soothing.

Baby-Led Sessions. Lifelong Keepsakes.

Each newborn session is completely baby-led, ensuring plenty of time for feeding, soothing, and cozy breaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our newborn sessions. Your baby's safety is our priority.

Ask a Question →

When is the best time to book?

+
We recommend booking during your second trimester to ensure availability. We will register your due date, and once baby arrives, we will finalize the session date within the first 14 days of birth.

What if my baby is fussy?

+
Don't worry! Newborn sessions are completely baby-led. We build in plenty of time for feeding, changing, and soothing. I have years of experience handling babies and keeping them comfortable.

When should we schedule the session?

+
The best time to photograph newborns is within the first 5 to 14 days after birth, when they are sleepiest and easiest to photograph in cozy wraps.

Can siblings and pets be included?

+
Absolutely! Siblings are a key part of the family story. Pets can also join for in-home sessions. We will capture family connections as well as solo baby portraits.
